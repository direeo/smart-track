A
Employee — On Track

Esther Ojo

Nexus Technologies Ltd
Your Role

You are Esther Ojo, a software engineer at Nexus Technologies Ltd. You are assigned to the Q2 Platform Sprint project and are well ahead of your code-review KPI target.

Username nex_star
Password emp123
Your Situation

You've been consistently ahead of schedule all sprint. You want to check how you are doing, run the AI forecast to see if you'll finish early, and log your latest progress update before your stand-up call.

Your Tasks
  1. Log in and go to your dashboard. How many projects are you on?
  2. Open the Q2 Platform Sprint project and find your Complete code reviews KPI.
  3. Click Run KPI Forecast — what does the system predict you will achieve?
  4. Click Behaviour Analysis — what is your risk level and recommended action?
  5. Log a new progress update: value 58, note "Two more reviews done — almost finished."
  6. Does the dashboard update to show your new value?

Employee — At Risk

Ngozi Ihejirika

Nexus Technologies Ltd
Your Role

You are Ngozi Ihejirika, a backend engineer at Nexus Technologies Ltd. You are assigned to the Q2 Platform Sprint project and have been struggling to clear your bug-ticket backlog.

Username nex_risk
Password emp123
Your Situation

Your progress has been slow — you've only resolved 25 of the 80 bugs required by the end of the sprint. You need to understand exactly how at-risk you are and what your options are before your next manager check-in.

Your Tasks
  1. Log in and navigate to the Resolve bug tickets KPI in Q2 Platform Sprint.
  2. How much progress have you made so far? What does the progress bar show?
  3. Click Behaviour Analysis — what is your risk level?
  4. Read the three recommended actions. Which option would you choose?
  5. Click Run KPI Forecast — does the system think you will meet the deadline?
  6. Log an update: value 28, note "Cleared 3 more this week."

Administrator

Sarah Okonkwo

Nexus Technologies Ltd
Your Role

You are Sarah Okonkwo, Company Administrator at Nexus Technologies Ltd. You oversee the whole organisation, manage user roles, and monitor overall performance.

Username nex_admin
Password admin123
Your Situation

A new team member has joined Engineering and you need to promote an existing employee to manager. You also want to check on overall KPI progress and add a new department for a growing business unit.

Your Tasks
  1. Log in and go to the Admin Dashboard. How many departments does Nexus Technologies have?

  7. Look at the overall dashboard — does it give you a clear picture of the organisation?
